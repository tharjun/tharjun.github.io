KDE Connect is on all major platforms.

Linux Desktop

KDE Connect Desktop Component is available in all major distribution packet managers. Non-Plasma users like Gnome and AppIndicator supported desktop users have various ways to enhance their experience like GSConnect which implements the KDE Connect protocol in GNOME and uses our android application, and indicator-kdeconnect is available for AppIndicator supported Desktops.
