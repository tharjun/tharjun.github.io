Hey, I am Konqi.

KDE Connect is on all major platforms.

Linux

Konqi and Tux are good friends. KDE Connect Desktop Component is available as a package on most Linux Distribution's repos. You can build it too. Non-Plasma users like Gnome and AppIndicator supported desktop users have various ways to enhance their experience like GSConnect which implements KDE Connect protocol to Gnome and uses our android app , and indicator-kdeconnect is available for AppIndicator supported Desktops.

That's why Konqi says, "Tux and me are good old Buddies!"

Android
